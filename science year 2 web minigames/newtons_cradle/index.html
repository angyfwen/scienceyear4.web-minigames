Newton's Cradle

how is this educational?

this simulation demonstrates several key principles of classical physics:

1. conservation of momentum: when one ball hits the others, its momentum is transferred through the stationary balls to the last one. ($p = mv$)

2. conservation of energy: energy changes from potential energy (height) to kinetic energy (motion). total energy remains constant in an ideal system!

3. elastic collisions: the metallic balls are nearly perfectly elastic, passing energy along with minimal loss to heat or sound.

4. damping: friction and air resistance are simulated via damping factors, causing the motion to eventually stop.
